# Trend Radar

The trend radar is the front door of the research system.

Daily and weekly research should start here before creating case studies. The goal is to identify new AI-to-C business-model signals, score them, and decide whether they deserve deeper research.

Folders:

- `signals/`: individual market observations.
- `research_ideas/`: questions or patterns worth investigating.
- `watchlists/`: products, companies, sources, categories, and search queries to monitor.
- `runs/daily/`: daily run notes.
- `runs/weekly/`: weekly synthesis notes.

Use Microsoft and Doubao as anchor examples, not fixed limits.
