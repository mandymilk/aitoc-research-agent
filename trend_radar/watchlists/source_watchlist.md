- Company pricing pages.
- Product documentation and support pages.
- App Store and Google Play listings.
- Product blogs and release notes.
- Investor relations pages.
- Regulatory filings.

## Market Data Sources

- Subscription app reports.
- App ranking and download analytics.
- Web traffic analytics.
- Survey reports with methodology.
- Public revenue or ARR claims.

- Consumer app monetization reports.
