# Trend Signal: Microsoft meters consumer Copilot with AI Credits usage limits

Date observed: 2026-07-13
Status: reviewed
Score: 4

## Signal

Microsoft's Copilot support page (last updated April 2026) documents that Copilot in individual Microsoft 365 plans (Personal, Family, Premium) is metered by an "AI Credits" usage-limit system, with Microsoft 365 Premium buying an extended usage allowance plus agents (Tasks, Analyst, Researcher, Photos Agent in preview), while business Copilot Chat exposes pay-as-you-go agents.

## Why It Matters

Even a company that can subsidize inference at scale is explicitly capping and metering consumer/business AI usage rather than offering flat unlimited access. Usage credits segment willingness to pay and protect gross margin from heavy users — a structural signal about AI-to-C economics, not a one-off pricing tweak.
