# Daily Runbook

## Objective

Find new AI-to-C business-model signals and decide whether they matter.

## Inputs

- Official product/pricing pages.
- Product blogs and release notes.
- App-store rankings and listing changes.
- Company announcements and investor comments.
- Credible tech/business reporting.
- Analyst or subscription-app reports.
- User-facing screenshots only when official sources are unavailable.

## Steps

1. Scan watchlist sources.
2. Identify signals related to AI-to-C monetization, retention, distribution, cost, or product behavior.
3. Create a trend signal for each meaningful observation.
4. Score each signal.
5. Promote high-scoring signals into research ideas.
6. Update product profiles only when a structured product field changed.
7. Write a daily run note even if no major trend appeared.

## Output

- `trend_radar/runs/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- `trend_radar/signals/YYYY-MM-DD-signal-slug.md`
- Optional: `trend_radar/research_ideas/YYYY-MM-DD-idea-slug.md`

## Scoring

Score each signal from 1 to 5:

- 1: weak anecdote or isolated noise.
- 2: relevant but low confidence.
- 3: meaningful single-product change.
- 4: strong signal with business-model implications.
- 5: market-shaping signal or strong thesis challenge.

Signals scored 4 or 5 should be reviewed in the weekly run.
