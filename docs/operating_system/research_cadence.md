# Research Cadence

## Weekly Loop

Goal: keep the evidence fresh and detect new AI-to-C trends before committing to deeper research.

Tasks:

- Scan official pricing pages, app-store listings, product blogs, investor comments, and credible market reporting.
- Add evidence notes for material changes.
- Add trend signals for new monetization, distribution, retention, or cost patterns.
- Promote strong signals into research ideas.
- Run freshness audit.
- Create or update falsification audit.
- Check case coverage so standalone challengers remain in scope.
- Update product profiles only when evidence changes a structured field.
- Write a short weekly finding if the evidence changes the research view.

Output:

- `data/evidence_index/YYYY-MM-DD-source-slug.json`
- `trend_radar/signals/YYYY-MM-DD-signal-slug.md`
- `trend_radar/research_ideas/YYYY-MM-DD-idea-slug.md`, when a signal is promoted.
- `findings/weekly/YYYY-MM-DD-weekly-digest.md`

## Monthly Loop

Goal: keep the analysis coherent.

Tasks:

- Review all new weekly evidence.
- Update product and company knowledge-base files.
- Refresh comparison tables.
- Re-score survival classifications.
- Record thesis changes.

Output:

- `findings/monthly/YYYY-MM-ai-to-c-market-review.md`
- `findings/thesis_updates/YYYY-MM-DD-thesis-update.md`, if needed.

## Quarterly Loop

Goal: create publishable research.

Tasks:

- Select one major question.
- Re-run case-study comparisons.
- Update unit-economic scenarios.
- Publish memo or deck.

Output:

- `reports/memos/YYYY-QN-topic.md`
- `reports/decks/YYYY-QN-topic/`, if a deck is needed.

## Event-Driven Loop

Trigger immediately when:

- A product changes price.
- A product adds ads.
- A product changes free-tier limits.
- A company bundles consumer AI into a larger plan.
- A company reports meaningful AI revenue, margin, usage, or retention.
- A major product is acquired, shut down, or pivots.

Required response:

1. Add evidence note.
2. Update product profile.
3. Decide whether a finding is needed.
4. Decide whether the main thesis changes.
