# Trend Signal: Shopee App goes live inside ChatGPT across 8 markets

Date observed: 2026-07-14
Status: draft
Score: 4

## Signal

Sea Limited and OpenAI announced (22 June 2026) that the Shopee app is now
available inside ChatGPT for users in Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines,
Singapore, Thailand, Taiwan, Vietnam, and Brazil — the first e-commerce platform
of its kind available in ChatGPT across Southeast Asia and Latin America. The same
partnership introduces ChatGPT for Business to Shopee sellers.

## Why It Matters

Product discovery is moving into a third-party AI assistant. If shoppers start
their journey in ChatGPT rather than the Shopee app, the AI platform may capture
the high-intent discovery and ad surface, and Shopee risks becoming a fulfilment
supplier behind someone else's agent. This is the central tension of the topic.
